About Loud Whistle

Our Purpose

Loud Whistle is a highly secure platform for whistleblowers to release disclosed information of unprincipled and unethical motives within the governmental or corporate hierarchy. In a democracy, entities are required to operate in a smooth and transparent manner. Loud Whistle keeps the public in loop while simultaneously ensuring the privacy of dutiful whistleblowers. etc.

Blockchain Security

Loud Whistle uses SHA-256 encryption to hash the personal information of the users and stores it on a Blockchain to guarantee isolation of this information. Having a decentralzied location for user data leads to the assurance of maximum privacy and safety.
